Frequently Asked Questions

Timesaver is a internet base enterprise that assist businesses, with the selling of products, goods and services. Timersaver acts as a middleman between retailers and it's users.

Registering a business is a quick and seamless process. During the registration process you will be asked for your business name, logo, email and banking credentials.

Users wishing to join the platform can simply sign-up, by creating an account with an email for verification.

Once your account has been created, there will be a register store link within the navigation bar. Click the link to follow the registration process. Note there are some documentation needed to complete the process, for this please reference "How do I connect my business?".

Once your account has been created, you will be able to set up your business perferences once satisfied you will be able to share the link to your business page via your favourite social media outlets, email and SMS messages.
